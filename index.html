Structure of Event

1) There will be 2 rounds- Prelims and Final.

2) Each round will have 2 segments - Auction Round and Transfer Window.

3) In the auction round participants will buy players from a pool provided by the organisers by spending the virtual money allotted to each team.

4) At the end of the auction round each team must have at least 5 players (for prelims) and 11 players (for finals).

5) Transfer window begins just after the auction round. Within the same pool, any team can approach any other team for exchanging players in return of virtual money/another player/player+money as it is done in the real world.

6) After a successful negotiation, both the seller team and the buying team must confirm the details of the transfer to the organisers and then the organisers will make the formal announcement of the transfer (in Fabrizio Style XD)

7) After both the rounds, each team will be evaluated on the basis of total player ratings and the money left in the wallet.

8) The team with highest points qualifies for finals/ wins the final.

9) In case of any disputes the decision of Jadavpur University Entrepreneurship Cell headed by the coordinators remains final.

10) Anyone not abiding by the aforementioned rules or doing something which is considered as against the code of conduct may be subjected to disqualification with/without a prior notice.
